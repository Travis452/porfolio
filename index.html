Void

Void is a personal project I am currently working on. This website is going to be for music artists, typically when you make music you end up with a bunch of unfinished projects that you may never use again and now they're just sitting in your folder untouched when maybe someone else could use that project that you made and put it to good use. Void will be a social media app for just that the artist will be able to upload the project/projects they dont like or know they aren't going to do anything with to the page where other artists can download said projects and put them to good use. This project is being developed using React and will be implementing Firebase.
